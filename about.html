About Thomas Padden

My name is Thomas Padden, and I am the creator of On Deck Media. I am a videographer focused on sports, interviews, documentaries, and creative video work. My goal is to create content that looks professional, tells a story clearly, and connects with viewers.

I enjoy combining strong camera work, editing, and visual style to produce videos that are engaging and polished. This portfolio was created to showcase my work, highlight my skills, and provide a professional space for potential clients to learn more about what I do.

Through On Deck Media, I want to present my work in a way that goes beyond social media by giving viewers a cleaner and more complete look at my projects.
