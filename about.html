About Me

My name is Thomas Padden, and I am the creator of On Deck Media. I am a videographer focused on sports, interviews, documentaries, and creative video work.

My goal is to create professional, high-quality content that tells a clear story and connects with viewers. I combine strong camera work, editing, and visual style to produce engaging videos.

This website allows me to present my work in a more professional way than social media, giving potential clients a better understanding of my skills and services.
